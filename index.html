I am a PhD student at Universidad Politecnica de Madrid, working with Prof. Roque Saltaren in the Robots & Intelligent Machines Group. My research interests lie Geometric Algebra for robots navigation, dynamics of Underwater robots and Microgravity robots.

I completed a Master degree in Automation and Robotic at the Univerisdad Politenica de Madrid and a Bachelors degree at the Universidad Militar Nueva Granada.

Actually, i'm research assistant at Center for Automation and Robotics, where working with underwater robots and robots for microgravity.
